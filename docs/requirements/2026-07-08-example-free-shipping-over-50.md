## What do you want?

Customers who spend more than $50 should get free shipping automatically at checkout.

## Why do you want it?

We want to encourage bigger orders and match what our competitors already offer.

## What does "done" look like?

When I add $50 or more of products to my cart and go to checkout, the shipping cost
shows as $0 automatically, with no coupon code needed.
